Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your international holiday with Travels Mantra.

Do you provide customized packages?expand_more

Yes. We create fully customised itineraries based on your destination, travel dates, budget, and trip type — honeymoon, family, friends, or group tours.

Do you help with tourist visas?expand_more

Absolutely. We provide tourist visa guidance, document checklists, application support, and travel insurance assistance for popular international destinations.

Can I book flights and hotels together?expand_more

Yes. Our complete packages include flights, hotels, airport transfers, sightseeing, visa support, and travel insurance — all in one plan.

What is included in the package?expand_more

Packages typically include flights, hotel stay, airport transfers, sightseeing tours, visa assistance, and meals (where mentioned). Exact inclusions vary by destination and package type.

What is the cancellation policy?expand_more

Cancellation terms depend on the airline, hotel, and package booked. Our team shares the full cancellation and refund policy before you confirm your booking.

How do I get the best quote?expand_more

Fill in the enquiry form with your travel dates, destination, and budget. Call us at 1800 2121 225 or email info@travelsmantra.com for a personalised INR quote within hours.
