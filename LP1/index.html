Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a holiday package? expand_more

Simply select your destination, fill out the enquiry form or call our experts. We'll guide you through the selection and payment process to confirm your booking.

Can I customize the existing packages? expand_more

Yes, absolutely! Every package can be tailored to your flight preferences, hotel choices, and specific sightseeing interests.

Is visa assistance included in all packages? expand_more

Most of our international packages include visa assistance. For domestic packages or specific countries, we provide guidance or full processing as per your requirement.

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Net Banking, UPI, and bank transfers through our secure payment gateway.

What if my flight gets cancelled? expand_more

Our support team is available 24/7 to help with rebookings or refunds as per the airline's policy and your travel insurance coverage.

Do you provide honeymoon inclusions? expand_more

Yes! Our honeymoon packages include special perks like room decoration, candle-lit dinners, and cake to make your first trip special.

Is Travel Insurance mandatory? expand_more

While it may not be mandatory for all destinations, we strongly recommend it for financial protection against unforeseen travel emergencies.
