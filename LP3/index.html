Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide customized packages?expand_more

Yes. Use our Build Your Package form or send an enquiry with your destination, dates, budget, and preferences. Our experts create a tailored itinerary for you.

Do you help with visa?expand_more

Yes. We provide tourist visa guidance, document checklists, application support, and country-wise visa information for popular international destinations.

Do you provide travel insurance?expand_more

Yes. We guide you on travel insurance options covering medical emergencies, trip delays, cancellations, and lost baggage.

Are flights and hotels included?expand_more

Most tour packages include flights and hotels. Inclusions are clearly listed on each package card with badges for Flight, Food, Visa, Sightseeing, Travel Guide, and Transport.

Can I build my own package?expand_more

Absolutely. Our Build Your Package section lets you specify destination, dates, hotel category, flights, visa, insurance needs, and budget for a fully custom plan.

How do I send an enquiry?expand_more

Use the hero enquiry form, Build Your Package form, or call 1800 2121 225. You can also email info@travelsmantra.com for a quick response.
